ABOUT US

Our Vision

At New Life City Church, our vision is to be a beacon of hope and love in our community. We strive to create an environment where people from all walks of life can find a home and experience the transforming power of Christ's love.New Life City Church is a dynamic and vibrant multi-ethnic church of God's people worshiping together in true fellowship with God and one another, committed to discipleship and various other ministries that enhance the coming of the Kingdom of God in the City of Tshwane, thereby becoming God's living instrument in reaching out the world for Christ.

Our Mission

New Life City Church is a dynamic and vibrant multi-ethnic church that seeks to bring people to Jesus and membership in God's family, develop them for their ministry in His Church life mission in the world,in order to magnify God's name. We want to spread the gospel message and impact our city and. We are committed to serving others, building strong relationships, and sharing the love of Jesus Christ.

Who we are

New Life City Church is a reformed multi-ethnic church in Sunnyside, Pretoria (South Africa), established as a church plant of the Reformed Church Brooklyn led by Dr. Brian DeVries, Benjamin Zulu and Malwande Mdonga in 2008. The church planting work continued under the leadership of Prof. Derrick Mashau and has since grown from strength to strangth. It was launched officially on the 09th of August 2014. New Life City Church is part of the reformed church family, and in particular the Reformed Churches in Southern Africa (RCSA/GKSA) denomination. NLCC is part of God's visible and invisible Church which He is assembling for Himself since creation.

Our Community

New Life City Church is a diverse and welcoming community of believers. We come together to worship God, build strong relationships, and grow in our faith. We invite you to join us on this journey of faith and discovery.
